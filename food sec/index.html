About India

India boasts one of the world's most ancient cultures, with its civilization tracing its roots back approximately 4,500 years. Widely regarded as "Sa Prathama Sanskrati Vishvavara," meaning the first and supreme culture globally, this accolade is bestowed upon it by the All World Gayatri Pariwar (AWGP) organization. Geographically, India is enclosed by the towering Great Himalayas to the north, extending southward until it tapers off at the Tropic of Cancer. To the east, it is bordered by the Bay of Bengal, while the west is embraced by the Arabian Sea, ultimately converging into the vast expanse of the Indian Ocean.
